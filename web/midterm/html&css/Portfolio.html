My first minor climb was the Lampanag ridge with the IVCF (Inter Varsiety Christian Fellowship). It was super fun that we start hiking at 8pm stayed overnight and had a fun with the falls in the morning.

My first ever mountain hike and it was a major hike Mt. Kitanglad. It is the fourth highest mountain in the Philippines and has an approximate height of 2,899 metres (9,511 ft). This is one of my biggest achievement in life.
